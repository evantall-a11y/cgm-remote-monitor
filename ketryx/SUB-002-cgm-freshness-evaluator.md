# SUB-002 — CGM freshness evaluator subsystem
The CGM freshness evaluator subsystem shall measure the elapsed time between current server time and the timestamp of the most recently received glucose reading and shall produce a staleness status of current, warning, or urgent based on user-configured staleness thresholds.
