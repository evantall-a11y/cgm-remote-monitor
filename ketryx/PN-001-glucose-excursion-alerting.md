# PN-001 — Patients and caregivers need to be alerted when blood glucose leaves the safe range
A patient or remote caregiver needs to be promptly alerted whenever the most recent continuous-glucose-monitor reading indicates a blood-glucose excursion or that the monitor is no longer producing trustworthy data, so that corrective therapy or device action can be taken before harm occurs.
