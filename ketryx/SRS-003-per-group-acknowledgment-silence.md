# SRS-003 — Per-group acknowledgment silence
When an alert at a given severity level and alarm group is acknowledged with a user-selected silence duration, the system shall suppress emission of subsequent alerts of the same alarm group and at or below the acknowledged severity for the duration of the acknowledged silence interval.
