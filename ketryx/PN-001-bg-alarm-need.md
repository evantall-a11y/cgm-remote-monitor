# PN-001 — Timely awareness of out-of-range and predicted blood glucose excursions

People with diabetes and their caregivers need timely audible and visual notification when continuous glucose monitor (CGM) readings indicate an out-of-range or rapidly trending blood glucose value, so that corrective action can be taken before the excursion causes harm.
