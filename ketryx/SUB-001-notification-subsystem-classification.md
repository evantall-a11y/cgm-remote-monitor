# SUB-001 — Notification subsystem evaluates readings and produces categorized alarm requests
The notification subsystem shall, on each evaluation cycle, compare the most recent continuous-glucose-monitor reading against the configured warning and urgent high and low thresholds and shall produce categorized alarm requests of severity Warning or Urgent for any threshold excursion.
