# SUB-001 — Real-time CGM data evaluation subsystem

The real-time CGM data evaluation subsystem shall be responsible for reading the most recent blood glucose entry, comparing its value against the user-configured threshold set, assessing the freshness of the entry, and producing structured alarm requests at the appropriate severity level.
