# PN-001 — Timely awareness of out-of-range BG and CGM data loss
A patient or caregiver monitoring continuous glucose data needs to be made aware in near real time when current glucose readings are outside safe range or when new sensor readings have stopped arriving, so that hypoglycemic and hyperglycemic events are not missed and corrective action can be taken.
